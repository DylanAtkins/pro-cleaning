For a professional, clean website that complements the minimalistic and clean images you've shared, here are some suggestions for font families, colors, sizes, and schemes:

---

### **Font Families:**
1. **Headings:**
   - Sans-serif fonts with a modern and clean look.
   - Options: 
     - **Roboto**
     - **Open Sans**
     - **Montserrat**
     - **Lato**

2. **Body Text:**
   - Sans-serif fonts for readability.
   - Options: 
     - **Nunito**
     - **Source Sans Pro**
     - **Arial**

3. **Buttons/CTA (Call-to-Action):**
   - Bold, slightly rounded sans-serif fonts for prominence.
   - Options: Same as headings for consistency or slightly thicker versions (e.g., Montserrat Semi-Bold).

---

### **Font Sizes:**
- **Headings:**
  - H1: 32px–40px
  - H2: 24px–30px
  - H3: 18px–22px
- **Body Text:** 14px–16px
- **Buttons:** 16px–18px, bold
- **Captions/Small Text:** 12px–14px

---

### **Color Schemes:**

1. **Primary Color Palette (Neutral and Professional):**
   - **Background:** #F7F7F7 or #FAFAFA (soft neutral white)
   - **Primary Accent:** #4CAF50 (muted green, symbolizing cleanliness)
   - **Secondary Accent:** #FFC107 (warm yellow for a friendly touch)
   - **Text Colors:**
     - Main Text: #333333 or #444444 (dark gray for readability)
     - Muted Text: #777777 (light gray for secondary info)

2. **Alternative Palette (Modern and Sleek):**
   - **Background:** #FFFFFF (pure white)
   - **Primary Accent:** #007BFF (calm blue for a professional feel)
   - **Secondary Accent:** #FF5722 (orange for energy and focus)
   - **Text Colors:**
     - Main Text: #222222 (nearly black for contrast)
     - Muted Text: #666666 (soft gray)

3. **Minimalist Palette:**
   - **Background:** #EFEFEF or #FFFFFF
   - **Primary Accent:** #A2B9BC (soft teal)
   - **Secondary Accent:** #B8A9C9 (light lavender)
   - **Text Colors:**
     - Main Text: #333333
     - Muted Text: #999999

---

### **Button and CTA Design:**
- **Button Background Colors:** Use the primary accent color.
  - Example: #4CAF50 or #007BFF
- **Button Text Color:** Always white (#FFFFFF) for contrast.
- **Hover Effect:** Slightly darker shade of the button background (e.g., #3E8E41 for green or #0056B3 for blue).

---

### **Imagery Integration:**
- Keep the images large and high-quality.
- Use soft shadows or borders (e.g., #CCCCCC) to separate images from the background subtly.
- Align the website’s overall tone with the neutral, professional look of the images.

---
